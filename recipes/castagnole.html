Castagnole




Description

Of Romagna origin, they are small pancakes that are fragrant on the outside and
soft on the inside, rolled in granulated sugar.
This cake is typical of carnival, and the most classic version involves the use of anise.

Tips

  • Before forming the balls you can let the dough rest for about 30 minutes. This way the flavors will blend perfectly.
  • If you do not like the taste of aniseed you can use schnapps, rum or orange juice.
  • Use powdered sugar mixed with a little cinnamon instead of granulated sugar.
